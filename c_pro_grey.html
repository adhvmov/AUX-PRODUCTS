C-Pro Grey

The C-Pro Grey air conditioners have been designed
in line with technological trends, ensuring
unparalleled user comfort thanks to microprocessors
with artificial intelligence that control the airflow.

C-PRO GREY

8,645 micro-holes
in the casing

Innovative
airflow system

Enhanced control and airflow comfort thanks to innovative 8,645 micro-holes in the front panel of the indoor unit. The dual micro-hole louver in the AUX C-Pro Grey air conditioner allows for precise airflow management in two modes: upper airflow and floor heating.

MICRO-HOLE TECHNOLOGY

In the C-Pro Grey series, AUX introduced a pioneering solution by combining dual-axis blades with a micro-hole panel in one device. The number of micro-holes has been increased to 8,645, which is three times more than the previous model. This has resulted in a larger surface area for releasing a gentle airflow.

Dual louver
with micro-holes

Upper Airflow

Setting the air conditioner's louvers upward directs the airflow toward the ceiling and walls, providing thermal comfort throughout the room.

Floor Heating

Setting the air conditioner's louvers downward allows warm air to be evenly distributed, creating a sensation similar to floor heating.

Gentle Breeze

Thanks to an increased number of micro-holes that enhance cooling efficiency, a perfect combination of gentle airflow and high cooling performance is achieved.

Sterilization at 57°C

During the self-cleaning process, the heat exchanger is heated to 57°C for an appropriate amount of time, eliminating microorganisms, bacteria, and viruses. The sterilization mechanism using 57°C is a key element that helps create an exceptionally healthy and comfortable indoor environment.

Humidity sensor

AUX C-Pro Grey air conditioners are equipped with a precise hygrometer for intelligent humidity detection in the room. When humidity is too high, the compressor frequency decreases to avoid condensation. When humidity is normal, the compressor frequency automatically increases to maintain temperature balance in the room.

Energy Efficiency Class A+++

The highest energy efficiency class in cooling mode. AUX C-Pro Grey air conditioners achieve an A+++ energy efficiency rating for cooling and A++ for heating mode.

Thanks to the use of a high-performance compressor with advanced inverter technology and the eco-friendly R32 refrigerant, AUX C-Pro Grey air conditioners reach the highest energy efficiency standards. They provide reliable, efficient operation while maintaining low energy consumption. These units meet all European Union energy-saving requirements.

NEW OUTDOOR UNIT

The front panel of the outdoor unit has been designed with performance in mind. The fan's round grille has been enlarged by 10mm, improving air exchange by 10%.

The vertically positioned motherboard is less susceptible to shock, dust accumulation, and damage.

Based on artificial intelligence, the unit's control algorithms enable reliable and economical operation of the air conditioner. Specially designed microprocessors, developed in the Japanese research and development center, feature enhanced computing power and smaller dimensions.

5TH GENERATION SELF-CLEANING

The upgraded self-cleaning system of the unit allows for the elimination of bacteria, viruses, mites, microorganisms, and dust particles. The health and comfort of your family are ensured through six stages of self-cleaning and sterilization, helping to deliver clean air.

The advanced sterilization system eliminates viruses, bacteria, dust, pollen, and microorganisms at a 99.9% effectiveness level.

New Wireless Controller

The wireless controller for the C-Pro Grey series air conditioners has been designed with comfort and ergonomics in mind. The display size has been increased by 67%, and a special coating on the display prevents mechanical damage.

CONDENSATE TRAY HEATER

The condensate tray heater prevents the accumulation of excess snow and ice in the unit. This ensures that the unit operates correctly even in extremely adverse weather conditions.
