The line is dedicated to people who appreciate economical, universal, but at the same time modern solutions. Minimalistic design with a wide range of functions at economic price.

FREEDOM PLUS SERIES 3

NEW GMCC COMPRESSOR

The AUX Freedom Plus Series 3 air conditioners are equipped with a compressor unit from the renowned brand GMCC (in AUX-09/12/18F3H devices). Enhanced compressor quality, operating at an ultra-high frequency of 120 Hz. Quick startup at low voltage and stable operation at high voltage

Performance and energy efficiency Increased efficiency motor unit by 5%

High reliability Oil circulation technology. High durability up to 20 years

Noise reduction Sound-absorbing materials reducing noise by 3 dB

For your health

The UV lamp ensures clean and healthy air. UV sterilization effectively purifies the air by eliminating viruses, fungi, and microorganisms. This function is particularly recommended for allergy sufferers and individuals prone to infections.

Honeycomb filter The honeycomb air filter effectively stops airborne contaminants such as dust and dirt.

Antibacterial filter Indispensable in removing all types of bacteria and pathogenic microorganisms. They are retained on the antibacterial coating on the filter surface. Thanks to the bactericidal substances found on the surface of the filter, the bacteria development is inhibited.

Self-cleaning function Thanks to the thorough self-cleaning function, the interior of the air conditioner is kept clean by eliminating moisture and inhibiting the growth of bacteria, mold and fungi

HIGHEST COMFORT

I FEEL function Intelligent "I FEEL" temperature interpreting enables perfect adjustment of the temperature to the conditions in the room, thus ensuring maximum comfort with low energy consumption.

Night mode When the sleep function is activated, the unit increases the set temperature by 1°C per hour during the first two hours of operation in cooling mode (in heating mode it lowers the temperature). For the next 5 hours, it keeps the temperature constant, after which the air conditioner turns off.

Turbo mode The turbo function allows you to cool / heat the room in a very short time by maximizing the fan speed of the indoor unit (using the highest speed of the fan).

Standby mode In standby mode, thanks to a specially designed PCB and disconnecting power from unused components of the unit, it is possible to reduce the electricity consumption of the air conditioner up to 0.3W per hour.
