Delightful, sophisticated design representing the highest level. The line is dedicated to people who appreciate a combination of sophisticated style and functionality.

THE WINNER OF THE IF DESIGN AWARD 2019

The IF DESIGN AWARDs are recognized internationally as a symbol of excellency in design. Nearly 60 specialists from over 20 countries select the winners based on clear and properly-defined criteria. The J-Smart series air conditioners were awarded the prestigious award in 2019.

Perfect design

Minimalism J-Smart Art air conditioners are dedicated to those who appreciate minimalist solutions. The state-of-the-art, white, matt casing adds lightness to the interior and becomes a "quiet background" for a subtle and balanced space of everyday life.

Modern style The modern style of the J-Smart Art series lies in the combination of excellent design, breakthrough inverter technology with the richest range of functions in its class. This model of air conditioners is one of the most interesting offers in the 2020.

Innovative design Easy-to-remove lower cover of the casing simplifies the installation and service of the device, and the patented air supply control system and ergonomic shape of the supply air louvers ensure optimal air distribution in the room.

The Air conditioning that cares about your health

UV Lamp Sterilisation The UV lamp effectively eliminates unpleasant odours and harmful substances from the air, ensuring the highest air quality in the room. UV sterilisation inhibits the growth of viruses, fungi, and microorganisms by 99% through efficient disinfection.

PM2.5 filter According to the World Health Organization, long-term exposure to PM2.5 results in a shorter life expectancy. J-Smart Art air conditioners are equipped with a PM2.5 filter, which removes fine dust particles floating in the air and removes 99.9% of these pollutants, ensuring clean and healthy air.

Antibacterial filter Indispensable in removing all types of bacteria and pathogenic microorganisms. They are retained on the antibacterial coating on the filter surface. Thanks to the bactericidal substances found on the surface of the filter, the bacteria development is inhibited.

High density filter The air conditioner is equipped with a high density filter that effectively stops airborne contaminants, including dust and dust particles.

Suitable for any interior

The J-Smart Art series air conditioners will perfectly match the style of any room in your home. J-Smart will provide the perfect temperature adapted to your lifestyle and style of the room. Enjoy optimal temperature at your home.

A wide range of operating modes

Night mode Automatic temperature control during sleep. The air conditioner increases the set temperature by 1°C per hour during the first two hours of operation in cooling mode (in heating mode it lowers the temperature at the same rate). For the next 5 hours, it keeps the temperature constant, after which the air conditioner turns off.

"I FEEL" function Intelligent "I FEEL" temperature interpreting enables perfect adjustment of the temperature to the conditions in the room.

Silent mode Exceptionally quiet operation mode in which the indoor unit of the air conditioner operates at a reduced sound pressure level, ensuring optimal conditions without excessive noise.

Heating mode The heating mode allows you to use the air conditioner all year round, even in the cooler months. Thanks to the heat pump, the device can heat the air in the room, being an alternative to traditional heating systems.

Drying mode Drying mode In the drying mode, the air conditioner lowers the humidity level in the room to the optimum value by automatically adjusting the temperature and airflow rate to achieve the best conditions. This prevents the growth of fungi, mold and bacteria.

Control the air conditioner no matter where you are

Built-in Wi-Fi module As standard the J-Smart series air conditioners are equipped with a built-in WI-FI module with a weekly programmer.

The application contains numerous functions, i.e. a timer, so there is no need for additional drivers.

WI-FI is a convenient solution for smartphone, tablet and computer users, enabling air conditioning to be controlled from anywhere 24h a day.

Controlling the air conditioner through the app is simple and convenient.
