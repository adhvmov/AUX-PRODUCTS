C-SMART PREMIUM ONYX

Innovative airflow system

The C-Smart series air conditioners have been designed with the aim of reducing the effect of air drafts. Thanks to the innovative system of micro-holes and specially shaped louvers, the air is blown out without the draft effect.

PIONEERING MICRO-HOLE TECHNOLOGY

To provide unparalleled airflow comfort, AUX designed the indoor unit casings and louvers to eliminate the sensation of drafts. This was made possible by 2,430 micro-holes in the C-Smart air conditioner. The expelled air is dispersed, ensuring thermal comfort, speed, and precision in maintaining room temperature.

Sterilisation at 57°C

The heat exchanger maintains a high temperature of 57°C for an optimal duration to ensure the best sterilisation effect, effectively eliminating microorganisms, bacteria, and viruses.

UV lamp sterilisation

The UV lamp effectively eliminates unpleasant odours and harmful substances from the air, ensuring the highest air quality in the room. UV sterilisation inhibits the growth of viruses, fungi, and microorganisms by 99% through efficient disinfection. C-Smart Premium air conditioners are equipped with UV lamps that guarantee clean and healthy air. This feature is particularly recommended for allergy sufferers and those prone to infections.

5TH GENERATION SELF-CLEANING

The upgraded self-cleaning system eliminates bacteria, viruses, dust mites, microorganisms, and dust particles. Your family's health and comfort are ensured through six stages of self-cleaning and sterilisation, delivering clean air.

The advanced sterilisation system removes viruses, bacteria, dust, pollen, and microorganisms with an efficiency of 99.9%:

NEW OUTDOOR UNIT

The front panel of the outdoor unit has been designed with performance in mind. The fan's round grille has been enlarged by 10mm, improving air exchange by 10%.

The vertically positioned motherboard is less susceptible to shock, dust accumulation, and damage.

Based on artificial intelligence, the unit's control algorithms enable reliable and economical operation of the air conditioner. Specially designed microprocessors, developed in the Japanese research and development center, feature enhanced computing power and smaller dimensions.

CONDENSATE TRAY HEATER

The condensate tray heater prevents the accumulation of excess snow and ice in the unit. This ensures that the unit operates correctly even in extremely adverse weather conditions.

New Wireless Controller

The wireless controller for the C-Pro Grey series air conditioners has been designed with comfort and ergonomics in mind. The display size has been increased by 67%, and a special coating on the display prevents mechanical damage.

The IFEEL function precisely adjusts the air temperature based on information collected from the controller."
