C-SMART PREMIUM ONYX

A+++ Energy efficiency class

The highest energy efficiency class in cooling mode. AUX C-Smart Premium air conditioners achieve a high energy efficiency class of A+++ for cooling mode and A++ for heating mode.

Thanks to the use of a high-performance compressor with advanced inverter technology and the environmentally friendly R32 refrigerant, AUX C-Smart Premium air conditioners achieve top energy efficiency parameters. They ensure reliable, efficient operation while maintaining low energy consumption. The devices meet all European Union energy efficiency requirements.

INNOVATIVE AIRFLOW SYSTEM

C-Smart Premium series air conditioners are designed to reduce the effect of air drafts. Thanks to an innovative system of micro-perforations and specially contoured louvers, air is distributed without creating a draft effect.

PIONEERING MICRO-PERFORATION TECHNOLOGY

To ensure an unprecedented level of airflow comfort, AUX has designed the indoor unit casings and louvers to eliminate the sensation of drafts. This has been made possible by 2,430 micro-perforations in the C-Smart Premium air conditioner. The expelled air is dispersed, providing thermal comfort while maintaining speed and precision in regulating room temperature.

5TH GENERATION SELF-CLEANING

The upgraded self-cleaning system eliminates bacteria, viruses, dust mites, microorganisms, and dust particles. Your family's health and comfort are ensured through six stages of self-cleaning and sterilisation, delivering clean air.

The advanced sterilisation system removes viruses, bacteria, dust, pollen, and microorganisms with an efficiency of 99.9%:

Influenza Virus

Escherichia coli

HFMD Virus

Staphylococcus aureus

Sterilisation at 57°C

The heat exchanger maintains a high temperature of 57°C for an optimal duration to ensure the best sterilisation effect, effectively eliminating microorganisms, bacteria, and viruses.

UV lamp sterilisation

The UV lamp effectively eliminates unpleasant odours and harmful substances from the air, ensuring the highest air quality in the room. UV sterilisation inhibits the growth of viruses, fungi, and microorganisms by 99% through efficient disinfection. C-Smart Premium air conditioners are equipped with UV lamps that guarantee clean and healthy air. This feature is particularly recommended for allergy sufferers and those prone to infections.

TEMPERATURE SETTING RANGE

The temperature control system allows you to set the range between 16°C and 32°C.
By pressing a combination of buttons on the wireless remote and setting the desired temperature range, the air conditioner will only operate within the selected range.
This feature is especially useful in rented spaces and properties where it's necessary to set a specific temperature range.

NEW OUTDOOR UNIT

The front panel of the outdoor unit has been designed with performance in mind. The fan's round grille has been enlarged by 10mm, improving air exchange by 10%.

The vertically positioned motherboard is less susceptible to shock, dust accumulation, and damage.

Based on artificial intelligence, the unit's control algorithms enable reliable and economical operation of the air conditioner. Specially designed microprocessors, developed in the Japanese research and development center, feature enhanced computing power and smaller dimensions.

CONDENSATE TRAY HEATER

The condensate tray heater prevents the accumulation of excess snow and ice in the unit. This ensures that the unit operates correctly even in extremely adverse weather conditions.

New Wireless Controller

The wireless controller for the C-Pro Grey series air conditioners has been designed with comfort and ergonomics in mind. The display size has been increased by 67%, and a special coating on the display prevents mechanical damage.

The IFEEL function precisely adjusts the air temperature based on information collected from the controller."

IN-BUILT WI-FI MODULE

The in-built Wi-Fi module allows you to control the air conditioner's operation from a smartphone, tablet, or computer connected to the internet, from anywhere in the world.

FAST COOLING AND HEATING TECHNOLOGY

Thanks to the use of an inverter compressor, the air conditioning system can quickly reach full efficiency, ensuring rapid attainment of the desired temperature in the room.

Based on artificial intelligence, the control algorithms ensure reliable and economical operation of the air conditioner. Specially designed microprocessors, developed in a Japanese research center, offer increased computing power and smaller dimensions.
