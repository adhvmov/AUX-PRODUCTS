UV disinfection at a temperature of 57℃

It inhibits the growth of viruses in 99% through effective disinfection.

The best models of the Q-Smart Premium series are equipped with UV lamps that guarantee clean and healthy air. UV sterilisation effectively cleans the air by eliminating viruses, fungi and microbes. This function is especially recommended for those who suffer from allergy and people susceptible to infections. The temperature of the evaporator piping is kept at the level of 57℃ for some time to ensure the best sterilisation effect.

ADVANCED FILTRATION

PM2.5 filter According to the World Health Organisation, long-term exposure to PM2.5 particulate matter results in a shorter life expectancy. Q-Smart Premium air conditioners are equipped with a PM2.5 filter, which filters fine dust particles floating in the air and removes 99.9% of these pollutants, providing clean and healthy air.

Antibacterial filter Indispensable in removing all types of bacteria and pathogenic microorganisms that are retained on the antibacterial coating of the filter surface. Owing to the bactericidal substances, bacteria development is inhibited.

High density filter The air conditioner is equipped with a high density filter that effectively stops airborne contaminants, including dust and dust particles.

WI-FI MODULE

As standard the Q-Smart Premium series air conditioners are equipped with a in-built Wi-Fi module with a weekly programmer. Wi-Fi is a convenient solution for smartphone, tablet and computer users, enabling air conditioning to be controlled remotely 24h a day. The application contains numerous functions, i.e. a timer, so there is no need for additional drivers. Controlling the air conditioner through the app is simple and convenient.

COMFORT FUNCTIONS

Silent mode

Exceptionally quiet operation mode in which the indoor unit of the air conditioner operates at a reduced sound pressure level, ensuring optimal conditions without excessive noise

„I FEEL” function

Intelligent “I FEEL” temperature interpreting enables perfect adjustment of the temperature to the conditions in the room.

Night mode

Automatic temperature control during sleep. The air conditioner increases the set temperature by 1°C per hour during the first two hours of operation in cooling mode (in heating mode it lowers the temperature at the same rate). For the next 5 hours, it keeps the temperature constant, after which the air conditioner turns off.

Heating mode

The heating mode allows you to use the air conditioner all year round, even in the cooler months. Thanks to the heat pump, the device can heat the air in the room, being an alternative to traditional heating systems.

Dehumidification mode

In the dehumidification mode, the air conditioner lowers the humidity level in the room to the optimum value by automatically adjusting the temperature and airflow rate to achieve the best conditions. This prevents the growth of fungi, mold and bacteria.
